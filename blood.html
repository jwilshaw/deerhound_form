Blood Sampling Instructions


Please ask a vet to take a minimum of 5mL blood and:

  • Fill a minimum of 5 red capped EDTA tubes to the line
  • Immediately invert the tubes several times
  • Label the 5 EDTA tubes with the patientâ€™s full name (including surname) and the date. If available, also label with the unique case number
  • Ensure the samples have not clotted (if clotted, please repeat the blood sample procedure)
  • Carefully package the 5 EDTA tubes into a Jiffy envelope, using tissue and a ziplock (or biohazard bag). Refrigerate the samples if they cannot be posted out the same day. Same day postage is preferable as these samples are for DNA analysis and DNA degrades over time
  • Post the packaged 5 EDTA tubes to:

    • Lorna Kennedy /Andrea Short, Vet Team (DH DCM Study),
    Centre for Integrated Genomic Medical Research,
    University of Manchester,
    Stopford Building,
    Oxford Road,
    Manchester,
    M13 9PT,
    United Kingdom.
N.B. If the 5 EDTA tubes cannot be posted the same day as collection please refrigerate the samples until posted.
